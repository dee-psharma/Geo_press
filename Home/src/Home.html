What is GeoPress?

GeoPress is a cutting-edge platform that makes local news location-aware and instantly actionable. We automatically extract geographic data from news articles, map real-time incidents, and deliver critical alerts during emergencies—helping communities stay informed and safe.

Location-Aware

The Problem We Solve

Traditional news lacks geographic context. When emergencies strike, finding relevant local information is slow, scattered, and frustrating.

No Geographic Context

News articles rarely include precise locations or maps, making it difficult to understand where incidents occur.

Slow Emergency Response

During crises, every second counts. Manual location searches delay critical decision-making.

Information Overload

Citizens are bombarded with news from everywhere, not filtered by what's actually near them.
